Pasta Salad

Several pastas tossed together with a vinaigrette and a variety of fresh, preserved or cooked ingredients.

Green Salad

Mixed greens, red leaf lettuce, green leaf lettuce and spinach all work well. it has the best combination of flavors and textures. It's crunchy and tangy, with creamy goat cheese.

Yogurt Salad

This refreshing Syrian-style creamy cucumber salad (Kh'yar bi laban) is made up of just 5 ingredients (including salt) and can be served as a dip, side, and party accompaniment!

Green Beans in Red Sauce

Passed down over the generations. Syrian Green Beans with Tomatoes are typically made with beef and served over rice pilaf, we also enjoy eating them as a vegetarian side dish.

Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Tender pasta in a luscious cream sauce with garlic and fresh parmesan cheese. A classic Italian-American favorite, alfredo pasta is easy to make at home with simple ingredients.

Shakshoukeh

A simple dish made of gently poached eggs in a delicious chunky tomato and bell pepper sauce.

Ma'amoul

Buttery date filled middle eastern cookies that will melt in your mouth and are utterly scrumptious. Naturally sweetened with dates these have minimal added sugar.

Qatayef

A sort of sweet dumpling filled with cream or nuts. It can be described as a folded pancake.

Date cookies

These special Syrian Date Cookies are known as Kleicha and have a smooth date filling that's wrapped in a buttery cookie and topped with toasted sesame seeds.
